# SICM EMS — Project Overview

**SICM EMS** (College Enrollment Management System) is a comprehensive platform designed to streamline the management of college events, student participation, and the automated generation of digital certificates.

## 🎯 Purpose
The primary goal of SICM EMS is to replace manual event tracking and certificate distribution with a centralized, automated system. It provides a seamless experience for administrators to organize events, teachers to manage their assigned events, and students to discover and participate in college activities.

## 🚀 Key Features

### 1. Multi-Role Dashboard
- **Administrators**: Full control over users, departments, events, and system settings.
- **Teachers**: Manage specific events they are in charge of, track attendance, and oversee certificate generation.
- **Students**: View upcoming events, register/join teams, track their participation history, and download earned certificates.

### 2. Event Lifecycle Management
- **Creation**: Support for department-specific or college-wide events.
- **Registration**: Individual and team-based registration workflows.
- **Attendance**: Real-time attendance tracking (registered, attended, absent).
- **Closure**: Automated or manual closing of events after completion.

### 3. Automated Certificate System
- **Template Engine**: Dynamic certificate templates with drag-and-drop capability for positioning fields (e.g., Student Name, Event Title).
- **Bulk Generation**: Generate hundreds of certificates for participants and winners with a single click.
- **PDF Delivery**: High-quality PDF certificates available for immediate download by students.

### 4. Communication & Notifications
- **Push Notifications**: Real-time browser notifications for event announcements and certificate readiness.
- **Mobile Responsive**: Fully functional on mobile devices for students to check events on the go.

## 🏢 Organizational Structure
The system is built around a hierarchical structure:
- **Departments**: Categorization of users and events.
- **Semesters**: Tracking student progress over time.
- **User Types**: Internal students (from the college) and External students (guests from other institutions).
